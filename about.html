About Us

WestSide Wings was developed by owner and CEO Tivona Abron. The idea that came about when she needed to create a project for her college JavaScript class. Tivona is a lover of flavor, and WestSide Wings continues that same motto. WestSide Wings has several locations throughout Southern California, and is looking to expand to Northern California, Texas, and Georgia.
